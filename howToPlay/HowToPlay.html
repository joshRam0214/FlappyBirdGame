Welcome to the the most amazing the best platform to play the one of the desired and anticipated games: Flappy Birds!!!!!. In this game you, the user, will simply press the space key bar to jump and guide you bird through the obstacles that will be coming at specific time intervals. Remember to carefully guide your bird through the obstacles because if you touch the obstacles then it will be game over for you. If you do not make your bird jump fast enough, then your bird will simply fall to the ground and it will be game over. After you you have played the game you can simpy entered a desired username you would like and see yourself on the Leaderboard of truth and see how you compare to all other competitive participants around the world.
